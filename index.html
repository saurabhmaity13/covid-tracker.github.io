About COVID-19

What is COVID-19? (Corono Virus)


COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. The new Virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China in December-2019.


Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause resiparatory infections ranging from the common cold to more svere Acute Respiratory Syndrome(SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.

COVID-19 Preventions

Wash your hands regularly for 20 secnds with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposal tissue or flexible masks when you cough and sneeze.

Avoid close contanct with people who are unwell.

Stay home and self-isolated from others in the household. If you feel unwell.

Stay informed by watching by news and follow the recommendated practices.

If you have fever, cough, breathing problems, etc seek medical care early.
